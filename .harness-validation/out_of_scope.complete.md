# Out Of Scope

Complete.

- GUI, web, daemon, or service work unless explicitly requested.
- Replacing Cobra, Bubble Tea, Bubbles, or Lip Gloss without approval.
- Business logic inside anonymous Cobra handlers or Bubble Tea `View` methods.
- Destructive filesystem or Git operations without approval.
- Network-backed features, telemetry, or secret handling without explicit scope.
- Broad rewrites outside the requested task.
